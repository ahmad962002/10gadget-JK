FAQS

Why do my earbuds not fit in my ear?

The earbuds may be too large for the ear canal, the ear tips may not be seated correctly in the ear canal, or there may be wax build-up.

Are there any earbuds that don't go in your ear?

Yes! There are earbuds that attach to your temple instead of going in your ear. They are called "Bone Conduction Earbuds."

What earbuds stay in your ears the best?

The best earbuds for staying in your ears are the ones that fit your ears the best. If the earbuds are too big, they will fall out of your ears, and if they are too small, they will be uncomfortable. You should also make sure that the earbuds seal in your ears so that the sound stays in and the noise from outside is blocked out.

How do you keep earbuds from falling out?

There are a few ways to keep earbuds from falling out. One way is to use the right size earbuds for your ears. Another way is to use the right size ear tips. You can also tighten the cord around your neck.
