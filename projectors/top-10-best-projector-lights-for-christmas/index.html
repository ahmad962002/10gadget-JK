FAQS

What is the best Xmas projector?

There is no best Xmas projector, as different people will have different preferences. Some people might prefer a projector that is small and easy to carry around, while others might prefer a projector with a lot of features. Ultimately, it comes down to personal preference.

How does a Christmas projector work?

Light from a Christmas projector is directed through a patterned slide. This slide is placed in front of the lens of the projector. The light shines through the small holes in the slide and creates a design on the wall or other surface behind the projector.
