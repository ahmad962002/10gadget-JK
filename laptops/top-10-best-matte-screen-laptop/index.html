    A couple of quick notes for those who are new to Windows laptops - if the keyboard fails to light up, check the F8 key - it can toggle the light on/off. FAQS

Is matte laptop screen better?

Matte laptop screens provide a good image quality and are less reflective than glossy screens. This makes them better for viewing in direct sunlight or other bright environments.

Which laptop screen is better matte or glossy?

Matte screens are preferred by many users because they don't produce reflections as glossy screens do.

Is matte screen better for eyes?

There is no scientific evidence that matte screens are better for eyes than glossy screens. Some people find matte screens less tiring to look at, but others find them more difficult to see in bright light.

Which laptop has best screen display?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as different people have different preferences for laptop screens. Some people might prefer a 4K display with vivid colors, while others might prefer a matte screen that doesn't reflect sunlight. Ultimately, it comes down to personal preference.
