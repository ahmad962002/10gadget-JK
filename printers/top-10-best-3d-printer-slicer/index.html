    -Parts don't stick: This happens when the printing bed is not level, or your temperature settings are off.     -Parts are "stringy" or "hairy:" exactly what it sounds like, your part finishes but has little spiderwebs of thing plastic strands on it, like a hot glue gun would leave. Rare but annoying.
