FAQS

What is the best desk for multiple monitors?

If you're looking for the best desk for multiple monitors, you'll want to find something with a lot of surface area. You'll also want to make sure that the desk is adjustable so that you can get the perfect viewing angle for each monitor.

Does 4 monitors increase productivity?

There is no definitive answer, as it depends on individual preferences and the types of tasks being performed. Some people may find that having more than one monitor increases their productivity, while others may prefer to stick with a single screen.

How do I make 4 monitors act as one?

You can use the nvidia-settings tool to create an "output configuration" that spans across all of your monitors. This will effectively make them act as one large monitor.

What size desk do I need for 3 27 monitors?

You will need a desk at least 80" wide to accommodate 3 27" monitors.
