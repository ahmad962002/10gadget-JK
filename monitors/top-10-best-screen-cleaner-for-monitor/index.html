While evaluating the many offerings of the booth, I noted a product called Screen Dr. Professional. The company has a variety of sizes for their Screen Dr. Line (2oz, 5oz, 9oz).     When you do remove the outer plastic lid, the microfiber cloth will likely be nested inside the lid. Slide a finger into the lid and gently remove the cloth. Remove the small plastic cap overlying the spray head. Spray the cloth with a mist of the solution. DO NOT SPRAY YOUR DEVICE! Use the cloth to wipe the screen, sides and back of your device. The instructions recommend to clean a cool screen, to reduce streaking. I have had no issues using this on my iPhone 6s Plus (inside of a catalyst waterproof case) and my iPad Air2 (inside of Otterbox defender) before use, during use or after use. This did not cause streaking. I have tried lens cloths alone, water, gentle wet wipes and I have never been satisfied with the way the screen looks. I really hate streaks. Do not use other chemicals/cleaners as they may have products that damage the coating on your devices/screen. Do not use wood based products as they are essentially fine sandpaper. The ScreenDr. formulation is safe for any device.
