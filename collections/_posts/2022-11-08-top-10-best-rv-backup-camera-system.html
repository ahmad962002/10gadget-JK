    On your main video cable that runs from the camera to the display there is going to be a little red wire on each end. What does this do and how do you install it? The instructions will not tell you. These two wires are what tells the display to turn on and off when the vehicle is in reverse. If you don't hook this up then the camera sucks and you have to manually turn it off all the time. Here's what you do... The end that is going to the camera, take that red wire and connect it to the red wire that is going to your brake light. This tells the monitor that power is going to the camera.
    On the monitor end you will have a little green wire attached to the harness that you will have no idea what it is for. This green wire hooks to the red wire on the other end of your video cable. This completes the relay between the two machines. After these are properly hooked up the camera and monitor it will turn on when you go into reverse and off when you go into drive or park.

FAQS

Are RV backup cameras worth it?

There is no one definitive answer to this question. Some people feel that backup cameras are worth the investment, as they can help to avoid accidents and potential injuries. However, others feel that the cost of a backup camera system may not be worth the benefits it provides.

What should I look for in an RV backup camera?

Some factors you may want to consider when purchasing an RV backup camera include: -the quality of the image -the ease of use -the size and shape of the camera -how well it is waterproofed

Which is better wired or wireless backup camera?

There is no clear cut answer as to which is better, wired or wireless backup cameras. Ultimately, it depends on the specific needs of the user. Wired backups tend to be more reliable and have a higher quality image, but wireless backups are easier to install and can be more discreet.

What is best wireless backup camera?

There is no best wireless backup camera because they vary in features, quality and price. It is important to do your research to find the one that suits your needs the best.
