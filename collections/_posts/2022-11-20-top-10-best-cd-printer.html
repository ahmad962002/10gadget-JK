For a typical 1-page, 1-sheet print job, the MX922 goes pretty fast -- certainly faster than my old printer. Duplex printing is slower, of course -- it's printing two sides of one sheet. But it was still steady and I suffered no paper jams or quality problems.

Of course, I always forgot to re-open that door ... but the folks at Canon anticipated this.
