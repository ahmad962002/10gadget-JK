    UPDATE 05/02/2019
    I have raised my rating by two stars, mainly because once I got past a particular problem, setup took five minutes.

    My problem was with the rubber earpads that come with the unit. I finally reached customer support, and their advice was dead wrong. I'm going to explain why it doesn't work, and then I'm going to explain how to make it work. These are instructions the manufacturer should provide.

    The rubber earpads come stretched over a plastic frame the same size as the earpiece. Carefully remove the foam pad from the earpiece. You will see a small speaker unit with three slots. The plastic frame that holds the rubber earpad has three corresponding tabs. Tech support said to push the plastic frame onto the earpiece, so that the plastic tabs on the frame go into the slots in the speaker, then twist clockwise to lock them in. I tried for an hour to make that work, with no luck.

    In desperation, I tried taking the rubber earpad off its plastic frame, so that I could see the tabs and get them lined up correctly. Doing that, it became obvious that the two plastic pieces simply could not fit together, and it occurred to me that the rubber earpad could go directly over the speaker frame. Voila! It fit perfectly. I took the foam pad I had removed from the earpiece and carefully stretched it back over the rubber earpad. I repeated the process for the other earpiece, and the problem was solved.

    I bought two of these units, intending to pair them together. The pairing is well-explained in the downloadable User Guide--much better than the paper quick-start that ships with the unit. It took only thirty seconds or so to pair the units, and it worked on the first try. So, now I'm in business.

    I am still of the opinion that this product is a great idea with a questionable execution and really poor technical support. But the workaround on the earpads got me over my biggest problem and, nw that the units are working, I think my wife and I will like them. We'll see how they hold up and, if I run into problems, I'll update this review.
