    In order to take advantage of the higher refresh rate you will need to open up your AMD or Nvidia software and create a profile that is set @75hz. Once this is created, go into display settings on your computer -> advanced settings -> monitor and then select the new 75hz profile from the drop down. Last, you will need to enable freesync from the monitor control panel (it's in the menu options) and then enable it in the AMD software (not sure about Nvidia). If you're lucky, you may be able to overclock the monitor to a higher refresh rate. Just create a profile for 80 or 85hz, select it in display settings and see if it takes. If it doesn't, the monitor will revert back to the previous refresh rate in 10 seconds. FAQS

Is 200 dollars good for a monitor?

Depends on the monitor. A high-quality, brand new monitor could be worth around $200, but a used or lower quality monitor could be worth significantly less.

Which monitor is best in low price?

There is no definitive answer to this question as it depends on individual preferences and budgets. Some monitors that are affordable and popular choices include the Asus VE248H, Acer G246HL, and ViewSonic VX2250WM-LED.

What monitor is best for 120 fps?

There is no definitive answer to this question as it depends on personal preferences and what type of games you plan on playing. Some gamers prefer monitors with higher refresh rates, while others find that they don't need anything more than a 60 Hz monitor. Ultimately, the best monitor for 120 fps gaming is the one that makes you happy.

Are cheap 144Hz monitors worth it?

Yes, 144Hz monitors are worth it. They provide a smoother gaming experience and make it easier to spot enemies and objects.
