FAQS

Which is the best camera for church live streaming?

A camera with a zoom lens is ideal for church live streaming, as it will allow you to capture shots of the entire congregation from a distance.

What is the best PTZ control?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the best PTZ control depends on the specific needs of the user. Some factors to consider when choosing a PTZ control include ease of use, functionality, and compatibility with other systems.

How much more beneficial is it to use a PTZ camera?

PTZ cameras offer a much greater range of motion than traditional fixed cameras, making it easier to capture all of the desired footage. Additionally, they can be panned and zoomed in on specific areas to get a closer look, which can be very useful during investigations or security operations.

How long do PTZ cameras last?

There is no definitive answer to this question as it depends on the specific camera model, how it is used, and other factors. However, PTZ cameras generally have a lifespan of 3 to 5 years.
