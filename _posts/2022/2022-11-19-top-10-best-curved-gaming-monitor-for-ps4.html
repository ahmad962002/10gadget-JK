FAQS

Can you use a curved gaming monitor with a PS4?

Yes, you can use a curved gaming monitor with a PS4, but it may not function as intended. The PS4 is not optimized to work with curved monitors, so some features may not work correctly.

What kind of monitor do you need for PS4?

A monitor that is compatible with the PS4 is needed in order to use the system. The PS4 supports HDMI, so a monitor with an HDMI port is recommended.

Is a curved monitor good for console gaming?

A curved monitor is not good for console gaming because the curvature can distort the image.

What size monitor is best for PS4?

A 27-inch monitor is the ideal size for a PS4. It will give you plenty of screen real estate to play your games on, and the larger size will make it more immersive. If you're looking for something smaller, a 24-inch monitor is also a good option.
