Do any outdoor security cameras work without wi fi?

Some outdoor security cameras work without Wi-Fi, but they may not have the same features as those that do require Wi-Fi. For example, some cameras that work without Wi-Fi may not be able to be viewed remotely.

Can you use a wireless security camera without WiFi but still get a live feed?

Yes, a wireless security camera can operate without WiFi but still provide a live feed. The camera connects to the security system through a hardwired connection.
