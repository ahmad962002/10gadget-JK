FAQS

Which monitor is best for studio?

For a studio monitor, it is important to find one that has low harmonic distortion so that you can hear the true sound of your music. The Yamaha HS8S has some of the lowest harmonic distortion levels on the market, making it a great option for studio monitors.

Is it worth buying studio monitors?

If you're a music producer, engineer, or just somebody who wants to hear your music the way it was meant to be heard, then yes - studio monitors are worth buying.

Are studio monitors worth it in untreated room?

Studio monitors can be helpful in an untreated room, but ultimately the room's acoustics will play a bigger role in the quality of your audio. If you're looking to improve your audio quality and don't have the means to treat your room, studio monitors are a good place to start.

Do I need 1 or 2 studio monitors?

It depends on your needs. Generally, one studio monitor is enough for most applications, but if you need greater stereo separation or a more accurate representation of the audio track, then two studio monitors may be better.
