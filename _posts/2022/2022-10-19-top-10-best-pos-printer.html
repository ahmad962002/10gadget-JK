    2. Download the printer APP to the phone (IOS and Android Avail)

    4. Start up the printer specific App, select the printer you want to edit, click settings and look for WLAN settings.
    5. Change printer WLAN from ADHOC access point to Infrastructure mode and fill in with details gathered in step 1
    6. Save and Send commits to the printer
    7. Printer should flash and if you view printers from a computer on the network you should see this printer available as a network printer if everything worked.
