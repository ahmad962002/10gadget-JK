Dialogue is in fact much clearer which could be an important feature for the hearing impaired who don't like to use closed captions. But the signal appears to drop the stereo effect. The voices sound like they are coming from the center of your head – in effect, a monaural signal. The music track, although softer, can still be heard, but it is unnatural and distorted. If I can hear music, I prefer it to sound natural. The result is annoying.

It was loud enough for me, but it might not be for some.
