FAQS

Which brand is best for laptop backpack?

There is no one "best" brand for laptop backpacks. Rather, it depends on what you are looking for in a backpack and your personal preferences. Some popular brands include JanSport, North Face, and Herschel Supply Co.

Which type of bag is best for laptop?

A laptop bag is a specific type of bag designed to carry a laptop. They are often backpacks or messenger bags with special padding and compartments for the laptop.

How can I carry my laptop without hurting my back?

There are a few different ways to carry your laptop without hurting your back. You can use a backpack, laptop sleeve, or roller bag.

What is the best way to carry a laptop?

There are a few options when it comes to carrying a laptop. The most common is to use a laptop bag or case. This will protect your computer from scratches and other damage. You can also use a sleeve to put your laptop in, which is less protective but easier to carry around.
